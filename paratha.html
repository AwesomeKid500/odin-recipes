Paratha recipe by Saad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Desi Ghee or oil (for cooking)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Salt (to taste)
  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to make dough.
  2. Divide and roll dough into thin rounds.
  3. Cook on a heated skillet with ghee/oil.
  4. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot.
