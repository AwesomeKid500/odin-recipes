Tea Recipe by Saad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 teaspoons loose black tea leaves
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 cardamom pods, lightly crushed (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small saucepan, simmer water, black tea leaves, and crushed cardamom pods for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add milk and bring the tea to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in sugar until dissolved.
  5. Simmer for an additional minute.
  6. Strain the tea to remove tea leaves and cardamom pods.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
