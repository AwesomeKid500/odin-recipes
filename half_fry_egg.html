Egg recipe from Saad

Description

Half fry eggs are a simple yet delicious breakfast dish. Prepared by frying eggs in a skillet with a small amount of oil or butter, they are cooked until the whites are set while leaving the yolk partially runny. The result is a heavenly combination of a creamy yolk and tender egg whites. Half fry eggs are often enjoyed with toast or bread, making them a comforting and satisfying start to the day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Cooking oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Crack an egg into the pan.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook until the white is set and edges are slightly crispy.
  5. Transfer the egg to a plate.
